1) Jostle - this means to push roughly (swaft-lee)

2) Slight - this means a small and than (ska-tuh)

3) Daunting - Frightening or scary (shelf)

4) Teenager - A person who is in their teen years (tee-ur)


Page-7

1) Aspiring Musician - a person who wants to become a musician (veek-luhgz)

2) Profoundly Deaf - absolutely deaf (kruhm-buhl-uhng)

3) Gradual - in phases (raaf-tuh)

4) Conceal - to hide (vi-noh)


Page-8

1) Specialist - a doctor specializing in a particular part of the body (flaw-ruhsh)


2) Deteriorated - worsened (buh-neeth)


3) Urged - requested (shuht-uh)


4) Impaired - weakened (sted-faast)



Page-9

1) Tear - a musical instrument with wooden bars, rows of them with different lengths (tee-urr)

2) Poking-Fun - this refers to the quality or ability to be developed (pokin-fuhn)

3) Roar - Utter a full, deep, prolonged cry (roar)

4) Praise - a person who plays the drums, tabla, etc. (preiz)
